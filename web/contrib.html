Contributing to NLTK

NLTK is an open source project, being developed by a small community of NLP researchers. NLTK is continually being expanded and improved. We invite contributions from interested members of the community.

There are several ways to contribute to NLTK:

  • Report bugs, Suggest features, or Contribute patches on Sourceforge.
  • Participate in discussions on the NLTK-Devel Mailing List, or in the NLTK Public Forums.
  • View our development schedule and offer to help out with one of our active or forthcoming tasks.
  • Submit your NLTK-based projects for inclusion in nltk_lite.contrib, or provide a new module for inclusion in core NLTK (must be relevant to NLP, substantial, original and well-documented). Please contact Steven Bird.
  • Translate NLTK tutorials into another language.
